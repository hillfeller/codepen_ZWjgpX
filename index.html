About

I am a budding programmer with solid Ruby on Rails foundations, interested in both front and backend web development. I'm on the lookout for new technologies, always troubleshooting and always learning. I previously worked at AvePoint as a senior technical analyst. While troubleshooting their proprietary application and observing its complex relationship with SharePoint, I worked with the development team to interpret the logs. It soon became clear to me that knowing the company's software wasn't enough - I would need to know and understand its underlying code. Subsequently, I enrolled in an apprenticeship with Bloc which provided excellent RoR foundations. This included CRUD actions, model-view-controller architecture, including but not limited to authenticating and authorizing users, signing in, and creating public profiles. Additionally, I focused on RSpec.

Projects

Whether the Weather

Whether the Weather serves as a user community for individuals that experience joint point during inclement weather.

In addition to providing weather updates for specific locations, users may also confirm local weather, as well as follow other users. Within the user profile a graph is generated based on the number of posts that they have assigned a weather label.

As joint ailments play a central role to the application, users can update their ailments.
